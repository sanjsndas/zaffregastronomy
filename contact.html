🌀 Frequent Gastronomic Inquiries

🔹 How can I schedule a private tour of the Zaffre Atelier?

We offer guided tastings and behind-the-scenes fermentation lab tours every Thursday and Saturday. Use the contact form and select “Culinary tour booking” — our concierge will confirm availability and provide fees (sliding scale for students).

🔹 Do you accept recipe submissions or collaborations?

Absolutely. The Matrix thrives on blue gastronomy innovations. Submit your original recipe with the “Collaboration & licensing” option; if selected, we feature it in our digital archive with full attribution and a co-created video tutorial.

🔹 I'd like to license the "Indigo Sourdough" technique for my bakery.

We offer creative commons for non-commercial use, but for commercial licensing please contact our legal kitchen team. We respond quickly with fair terms and technical support.

🔹 Can I request a custom blue-themed menu for an event?

Yes. The Matrix provides remote consulting and on-site pop-ups (limited to US & EU). Send details (guest count, dietary restrictions, preferred blue pigment intensity) and we will draft a bespoke zaffre menu.

🔹 What is the average response time for press interviews?

Within 12 hours for urgent press and 24 hours for standard. We have a dedicated media kit with high-res images of cobalt dishes, chef bios, and the full history of our 58k word gastronomic encyclopedia.
