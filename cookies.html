🍪 Cookies & Similar Technologies
The Zaffre Cookie Ethos — absolute minimalism, total transparency

We believe that your online path through our cobalt kitchen should be free of invisible surveillance. This Cookies Policy explains the only technology we use: an essential session cookie that vanishes when you leave. No analytics, no trackers, no fingerprinting, no surprises.

1. Do We Use Cookies? — The Short Answer

Yes, but only one strictly necessary session cookie. Zaffre Gastronomy Matrix does not deploy any advertising, performance, analytics, or third-party cookies. We have zero interest in tracking your browsing behavior across websites. Your privacy is an ingredient we never compromise.

🔒 Zero-Tracking Promise: No Google Analytics, no Meta/Facebook pixel, no TikTok SDK, no Hotjar, no mouse tracking, no fingerprinting libraries, no persistent cross-site identifiers. The matrix runs on pure code and respect.
2. Essential Session Cookie — The Only Exception

To enable certain core functionalities of our website — namely the anti-abuse protection for our contact forms and to maintain a seamless session for users — we place a minimal session cookie. Here are the technical details:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
zaffre_session (or similarly anonymous identifier) Prevents form spam and CSRF attacks; ensures that when you fill out the contact or workshop form, your request is processed securely. Also maintains brief session state — no personal data is stored inside this cookie. Session only — expires immediately upon closing the browser (or after 2 hours of inactivity). Strictly Necessary

This cookie does not recognize you across different devices, does not store your name, email, or any preferences, and cannot be used for behavioral profiling. Without this cookie, some security features of our forms would be impaired.

3. No Third-Party Cookies — Absolute Independence

Unlike the vast majority of websites, we do not embed content from external platforms that would drop their own cookies. Specifically:

  • No embedded YouTube or Vimeo — any video content is self-hosted or uses privacy-enhanced no-cookie modes (but currently, we simply do not embed third-party videos).
  • No Google Fonts, no external font CDNs — all typography is loaded locally from our server.
  • No social media sharing buttons that include trackers (e.g., AddThis, ShareThis). We provide clean, manual links without tracking parameters.
  • No advertisements, affiliate networks, or retargeting pixels — we are a gastronomic matrix, not a data broker.

Therefore, when you visit Zaffre Matrix, no external company receives any information about your visit via cookies.

4. Local Storage, IndexedDB, and Fingerprinting — Zero Usage

We do not store any information in your browser’s localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB for tracking purposes. There is no hidden fingerprinting mechanism (canvas, WebGL, audio, or device fingerprinting). We also abstain from using ETag or HTTP cache-based tracking.

🧼 Clean slate design: Zaffre's engineering team follows “privacy by default”: we never read or write any data to client-side storage except the minimal session cookie described above.
5. Cookie Consent & Your Control

Because we only use strictly necessary session cookies that are exempt from consent requirements under the ePrivacy Directive and GDPR, we do not display a pop-up cookie banner. However, we value your autonomy. You may at any time:

  • Disable cookies globally via your browser settings (note: disabling session cookies may break the contact form anti-spam mechanism, but you can still reach us via email at privacy@zaffrematrix.com).
  • Clear your session cookies after browsing — simply close your browser or clear cookies in your privacy settings.
  • Use private/incognito mode — the session cookie will be isolated and automatically erased once you close the private window.

For more information about how to manage cookies in your browser, visit the help section of your specific browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).

🍽️ Your cookie preferences

Since we use no tracking or analytics cookies, there’s nothing to opt out from, except the essential session cookie required for security.

6. Analytics & Performance — Nonexistent

Zaffre Gastronomy Matrix does not use any analytical tool such as Google Analytics, Matomo, Plausible, or similar. We deliberately choose not to measure page views, bounce rates, or user journeys. The matrix exists to inspire and inform, not to convert behaviors into data points. The only technical logs we keep are server access logs (IP addresses, timestamps, requested URLs) used exclusively for security and troubleshooting — these logs are automatically purged every 14 days and are never correlated into user profiles.

If we ever decide to implement anonymous, privacy-preserving analytics (e.g., self-hosted, no cookies, aggregated counts only), we will update this policy and provide a clear, opt-in mechanism. As of now, there is zero tracking.

7. Third-party links and external resources

Our website may occasionally contain links to external culinary references, academic papers, or specialty ingredient suppliers. Those external websites operate under their own privacy and cookie policies. Zaffre Matrix has no control over their tracking technologies. We encourage you to review those policies before interacting with external sites. Clicking on such links does not cause any cookies to be set from our side.

8. Do Not Track (DNT) & Global Privacy Control

Because we do not track users across sites or engage in targeted advertising, our website already honors the spirit of Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control. Your browser’s DNT header is respected automatically — we ignore no signals. If you have enabled Global Privacy Control or similar opt-out mechanisms, you are already benefiting from the highest level of privacy we offer.

9. Updates to this Cookies Policy

Transparency is a living commitment. We may revise this Cookies Policy to reflect improvements in our privacy practices or changes in legal obligations. The “Last Updated” date indicates the latest version. If we ever contemplate introducing optional non-essential cookies (which is extremely unlikely given our ethos), we will notify all active subscribers via email and request explicit consent before deployment.

Any changes will be minor and will never introduce hidden trackers. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.
