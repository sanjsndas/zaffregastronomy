⟡ Privacy & Data Stewardship ⟡

The Zaffre Gastronomy Matrix respects your digital autonomy. We do not harvest behavioral data, sell personal information, or embed third-party trackers. This policy outlines our minimal data handling, your rights, and our pledge to gastronomic confidentiality.

1. Information We Collect — The Minimal Blueprint

As part of our culinary atelier philosophy, we collect only what is necessary to respond to your inquiries, manage bookings, or deliver matrix services. Our data categories include:

  • Direct identifiers: name, email address, phone number (only when you voluntarily provide them via contact form, newsletter registration, or workshop sign-up).
  • Correspondence data: any message, recipe request, or partnership details you submit through our encrypted contact forms or direct email.
  • Reservation/transaction data: if you book a culinary tour or purchase a workshop ticket, limited payment confirmation data (processed by PCI-compliant partners, we never store full payment credentials).
  • Technical minimal logs: server logs may retain IP addresses for security (e.g., brute force protection). These logs are automatically purged every 14 days and never analyzed for tracking.
🧿 Zero-Tracking Pledge: No cookies for advertising, no analytics scripts, no social media pixels. We don't fingerprint your browser. Our matrix runs without Google Analytics, Meta SDK, or similar surveillance tools.
2. How We Use Your Information — Gastronomic Purpose Only

Your data serves the sole purpose of facilitating the Zaffre experience:

  • Responding to your inquiries (e.g., recipe deep-dives, atelier visits, press collaborations) within 24 hours.
  • Sending occasional updates about workshops, blue cuisine events, or new matrix releases — provided you have explicitly opted in. Every email contains a one-click unsubscribe link.
  • Processing ticket purchases for “Blue Fermentation Workshops” or physical tours, ensuring delivery of digital assets or access confirmations.
  • Improving our culinary knowledge base (aggregated and anonymized — e.g., “most requested recipe category”). We never build individual profiles.

We will never use your data for automated decision-making or any form of behavioral advertising. The Zaffre Atelier does not rent, sell, or trade personal information.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & Global Alignment)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar frameworks, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you submit a contact form, sign up for newsletters, or register for an event, you actively consent to the processing required.
  • Legitimate interests: Responding to communications, protecting against fraud, and ensuring security of our digital atelier.
  • Contractual necessity: For paid event registrations, processing is necessary to fulfill our agreement.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting privacy@zaffrematrix.com or using the “unsubscribe” option in emails.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties — Limited & Trusted

Zaffre Gastronomy Matrix does not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Only essential third-party services with strict data protection commitments are involved:

  • Email relay & storage: We use end-to-end encrypted email services (ProtonMail / self-hosted secure relays). Messages are stored only as long as needed for ongoing correspondence.
  • Payment processors: Stripe or PayPal for event bookings. These processors are PCI DSS Level 1 certified and do not share data with us beyond transaction confirmation.
  • Secure hosting: Our website resides on EU-based servers (climate-neutral provider) with strict access controls. No external CDN that injects trackers.

We never transfer your data internationally without equivalent safeguards. Any data transfers are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

6. Data Retention — Gastronomic Temporality

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the original purpose:

  • Contact form submissions: stored for up to 2 years after last interaction, then securely anonymized or deleted.
  • Workshop registration data: retained for 3 years for tax/attendance records, then deleted.
  • Email newsletter list: you remain on the list until you unsubscribe; we delete unsubscribed records permanently within 48h.
  • Server logs: IP addresses purged every 14 days automatically.

If you request deletion, we remove identifiable data within 30 days, except where legal retention obligations apply (e.g., financial records for 7 years in some jurisdictions).

7. Children’s Privacy — Blue Garden Guidelines

The Zaffre Matrix does not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 16. Our culinary content is family-friendly, but any user under 16 must have parental/guardian consent to contact us. If we discover that a child’s data has been collected without verifiable consent, we delete it immediately. Parents/guardians may request removal by contacting privacy@zaffrematrix.com.

8. Cookies & Similar Technologies — Almost None

Our website does not use tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or local storage for advertising. We only use:

  • Essential session cookie: if you use our contact form to prevent abuse (no persistent identifier, expires when browser closed). No personal data stored.
  • No third-party cookies: we don’t embed YouTube videos, Google Fonts, or external assets that drop cookies. All fonts are local.

See our Cookies Policy for complete transparency (it’s intentionally minimal).

9. Security Measures — Matrix Encryption Protocols

We protect your data with industry-standard safeguards:

  • Full TLS 1.3 encryption (HTTPS) across the entire website.
  • End-to-end encryption for internal email communication concerning sensitive inquiries.
  • Regular security audits, automated patching, and strict access controls to databases.
  • All passwords are hashed using modern algorithms (bcrypt) where applicable.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and supervisory authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

10. International Transfers

The Zaffre Matrix is based in the USA with servers in the EU for privacy optimization. For data transfers from the EEA to the US, we rely on adequacy mechanisms and Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) plus additional technical safeguards. Whenever we engage with third parties, we ensure they match our privacy standards. You may request details of transfer safeguards by emailing us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Last Updated” date at the bottom of this page indicates the latest revision. If we make material changes that affect your rights, we will notify you via email (if you are in our contact database) or via a conspicuous notice on our website. Your continued use of the site after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
